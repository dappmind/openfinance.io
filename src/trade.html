The Power of
Blockchain Technology

From ledger maintenance and smart contracts to the 'tokenization of securities', we at OpenFinance Network knew that blockchain technology could streamline the alternative trading process in a truly revolutionary way.

The Ethereum blockchain makes keeping track of ownership easy. Its shared source of truth means that it cannot be hacked by outsiders or manipulated by insiders.

To securely and successfully verify transactions on the Ethereum chain, the chain requires users to cover the cost of 'Gas' - the fee for conducting trasnaction on the chain.

Openfinance Network utilizes a hybrid DEX modal that has off-chain matching and on-chain peer-to-peer settlement.

Investor Passports

We also knew that a massive upfront cost in the alternative trading lifecycle was simply the time it took to review documentation and paperwork for our users to be legally compliant. While this seemed like an obvious function that machines could perform better than humans, other players in the industry were lagging behind, and investors suffered as a result.

We solved this by streamlining the process with a tool called the Investor Passport, which collects and analyzes user information and documentation to ensure that users are authorized to trade. This not only speeds up the process of obtaining legal compliance, but it greatly reduces the friction and overhead of hiring rooms full of people to review documentation for our users.
