OpenFinance Token Questions

OFN is a Security token WITH utility. Offered under Regulation D, S and Planned A+

The token acts as a perpetual license to use the OFN technology system. Similar licenses and subscriptions can run up to $10,000 a month for access to other proprietary clearinghouses or electronic communication networks in the financial markets industry. The target users of the tokens are the 634,000+ brokers across 3,700 firms; 200,000+ investment advisors across 34,000 firms; 60M+ individual investors; and the developers who build products for this market.
There is a 10% Quarterly dividend paid out to token holders on Gross Adjusted Revenue.

In addition to the utility of the token, holders receive at 100 BP reduction on trading fees.
The capacity algorithm is designed in such a way to allow the network to continue functioning when all staked tokens have been used up. If the amount of trades double over a staking period, the next period allows for more trades in response to this increase. This ensures that even if tokens are not being used on the network, those staked in the ecosystem get access to the proper amount of trades to keep the platform running smoothly.
Excess capacity may also be leased within the network, in increments of staking periods. This allows for institutional holders to gain income on extra tokens they may hold, and active traders to temporarily increase their own capacity when they reach their own limit before the end of the staking period.
